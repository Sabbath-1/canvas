The Ultimate Chocolate Cake Recipe

There's nothing quite like the smell of a freshly baked chocolate cake wafting through your kitchen. This recipe has been perfected over years of testing and is guaranteed to deliver a moist, rich, and incredibly delicious chocolate cake.

Why This Recipe Works

What sets this chocolate cake apart is the combination of high-quality cocoa powder, the perfect balance of wet and dry ingredients, and a secret ingredient that keeps it incredibly moist for days.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add buttermilk, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla to the flour mixture and mix until well combined.
  4. Carefully stir in boiling water until the batter is smooth.
  5. Pour batter evenly into prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Let cool in pans for 10 minutes before removing to wire racks to cool completely.
Pro Tip: For an extra moist cake, brush the cooled layers with simple syrup before frosting.

Frosting Options

While this cake is delicious on its own, pairing it with the right frosting takes it to the next level.

Chocolate Buttercream

A classic choice that never disappoints. Creamy, rich, and perfectly chocolatey.

Cream Cheese Frosting

The tanginess of cream cheese balances the sweetness of the cake beautifully.
